How to Play

Choose your weapon: Rock, Paper, or Scissors. The computer will also pick one at random. The rules are simple — Rock beats Scissors, Paper beats Rock, and Scissors beat Paper. If both choices are the same, it's a tie. The first to reach five points wins the game!
